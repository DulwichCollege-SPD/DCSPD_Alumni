Alumni Reflections

“The thing I love the most about Dulwich is the sense of community and it never goes away no matter where I am.”


Justine P.

Dulwich Alumnus 2019 - 2023

“Dulwich will always be part of who I am. It shaped the way I think, learn, and connect with others. The values I took with me, including international mindedness, and the academic foundation I received have guided me every step of the way. Not only do I feel proud of its high standard, but also of the sense of community and family the moment we step through its doors. I entered it not even knowing how to speak English, and left full of treasured memories.”

Emma P.

Dulwich Alumnus 2019 - 2023

“As a parent and a neighbour for 4 years, my simple but warm memories of Dulwich are the special mood you get when you can walk your kids to or from school, the vibe around the gates at exit time and the magnificent musical and production shows when we were privileged to be inside the school and get to feel the unique atmosphere. Sometimes wishing we were students inside Dulwich ourselves, back to school!”

Nicolas

Emma & Justine’s dad

“Besides the great academic and personal enrichment Dulwich has offered my daughters, it has also given me, as a parent, so many wonderful memories and strong friendships. From the supportive and enthusiastic teaching team and lovely students to the wonderful mums I had the joy of organising and sharing school events with, this community has left a lasting imprint on my heart.”


❤️ Sylvie

Emma & Justine’s mum

“My best memories of Dulwich are the excitement of running to the lunch hall for a sandwich and hurrying out to play football with all my friends.”

James C.

Dulwich Alumnus 2009 - 2020

“I’ve been at Dulwich since toddler all the way to Year 13. My favourite memory here is probably [Senior Prank Day], when we decorate all our teachers’ classrooms with various themes.”

Felix Z.

Class 2024

“My favourite memory at Dulwich is performing Johnny B. Goode at the Graduation Ceremony. It was the first big performance I did for the school. Definitely one of my most memorable moments.”


Josh A.

Class 2024

Giving back to the community

Calling All Alumni: Help Us Connect!

- Become an Alumni Representative -


Dear DCSPD alumni:


The Community needs you!


We are seeking enthusiastic and dedicated Alumni Representatives to help strengthen our global network and foster a deeper connection within our community.


To start, we are hoping to find Reps in the following regions:

United States (East and West), United Kingdom (covering Europe),

Singapore (covering Asia), Australia, and Canada.


As an Alumni Rep, you are excepted to:

  • Serve as the main point of contact for Dulwich alumni in your region.

  • Gather feedback and concerns from alumni.

  • Collaborate with the alumni coordination team when organizing alumni events.

  • Document alumni reunions by taking photos or videos and sharing them with us.


If you’re interested or would like more information,

please contact Noa.Zhang@Dulwich.org
